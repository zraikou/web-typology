INFJ

The Advocate

INFJs are creative, gentle, and caring. They seek meaning and connection in ideas, relationships, and material possessions. They want to understand what motivates people and are insightful about others.

Strengths

  • • Creative and insightful
  • • Principled and value-driven
  • • Dedicated to helping others
  • • Good with language and communication

Growth Areas

  • • Sensitive to criticism
  • • Can be too perfectionistic
  • • May have difficulty with practical matters
  • • Tendency to burnout from helping others
